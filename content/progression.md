## Velious

To unlock Luclin, you must kill:

- Wuoshi
- Zlandicar
- Klandicar
- Lord Yelinak
- Hraasha the Warder
- Nanzata the Warder
- Tukaarak the Warder
- Ventani the Warder

## Luclin

To unlock PoP, you must kill:

- Thought Horror Fiend
- Grief Veneficus
- Insanity Crawler
- Xerkizhh the Creator
- Emporer Ssraeshza

## Planes of Power

To unlock Omens of War, you must kill:

- Saryn
- Quarm
